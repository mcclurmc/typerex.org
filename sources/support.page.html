TypeRex is an Open-Source software

TypeRex is an open-source software developed by OCamlPro. We plan to enlarge supported platforms (Eclipse, windows, ...) in the next releases, but if you really need a specific platform support, please contact us and we will be happy to discuss about your needs.

  • You can ask your questions about TypeRex on Stack Overflow. Make sure to tag your question with at least tags TypeRex and OCaml.
  • Bugs reports should be sent through the github issue tracker..

Industrial Users

If you are a company and you are using TypeRex, drop us a mail at OCamlPro and we will be very happy to get your feedback on our tools. Moreover, we can help you if you are looking for:

  • training on TypeRex
  • targeted development efforts (typically for supporting new platforms)
  • and long-term commercial support
